About me

MERN Stack Developer


Hello! I'm Sridevi, a passionate MERN stack developer who loves building modern, scalable, and user-friendly web applications. From intuitive front-end interfaces to robust back-end APIs, I enjoy crafting seamless digital experiences that solve real-world problems.

My journey began with curiosity about how websites work, which led me from HTML & CSS to mastering JavaScript and frontend libraries like React.js. As I dove deeper, I discovered the power of backend technologies like Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, allowing me to build full-stack applications with end-to-end functionality.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at VIT Vellore, where I've developed a solid foundation in data structures, algorithms, OOPs, databases, operating systems, and computer networks. I'm always eager to learn, collaborate, and contribute to impactful tech projects.
